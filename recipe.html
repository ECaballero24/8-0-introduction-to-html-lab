THE BEST PASTRAMI SANDWICH

Description

The pastrami and swiss cheese sandwich is my favorite sandwich. I eat it at least once a week from my local deli. It is absolutely amazing!

Ingredients

  • 6 slices Saputo Swiss cheese
  • 2 slices rye bread
  • 10 thin slices of pastrami
  • 60 mL(1/4 cup) Dijon mustard
  • 160 mL(2/3 cup) sauerkaurt

Directions

  1. Toast 2 slices of rye bread.
  2. Lay both slices of bread next to eachother.
  3. With a butter knife, spead a 1/8 inch layer of Dijon mustard on both sides of the toasted bread.
  4. Add 10 slices of pastrami to one side of bread.
  5. Add 6 slices of Saputo Swiss cheese on top of the pastrami.
  6. Add 160mL of sauerkaurt to other slice of bread.
  7. Carefully, place the two halves of bread together.
  8. Lastly, with a sharp knife, cut the sandwich in half.
  9. Enjoy!!
